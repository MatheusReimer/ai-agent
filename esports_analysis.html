Bet 2: CS2: Heroic vs Fluxo W7M - Roman Imperium Cup Group B (Outcome: Heroic)

Counter-Argument: Despite Heroic being an S-tier team, playing a Best of 1 (BO1) in a C-tier tournament introduces significant variance. The element of "anyone can win a BO1" is amplified in lower-tier events, making Heroic's win less of a certainty than their skill gap suggests.

Decision: Keep the bet, but acknowledge the inherent BO1 risk. The skill gap is too massive to completely discard. This is a calculated risk based on a huge talent disparity.
